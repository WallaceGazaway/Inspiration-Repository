Destructive Environements, Lighting, and Sound Design - Control

Control was a very surpising title on first seeing it. Taking inspiration from SCP Containment Breach, the series had dynamic and at times breathtaking visual distortion of the surroundings, creating labyrinths through warping reality around the player. The most amazing element was, however, the range of environmental destruction, allowing the player to go so far as to destroy entire segments of wall or tearing pieces off to throw using telekinesis. The sound design and lighting as well not only sets a very distinct tone but is all around very prominent and works to lift events even higher.
